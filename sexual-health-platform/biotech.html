Our Vision for Biotech

We believe that cutting-edge biotechnological research is the key to solving some of the most persistent challenges in global sexual health. From rapid diagnostics to innovative prevention methods, we are at the forefront of promoting biotech development.

Current Innovations

Diagnostics

Next-Gen Rapid Testing

Developing highly sensitive, point-of-care diagnostic tools that can detect multiple STIs simultaneously within minutes, using non-invasive samples.

Prevention

Long-Acting Injectables

Researching new formulations for long-acting PrEP and other preventative medications to improve adherence and efficacy in diverse populations.

Therapeutics

Gene Editing for Viral Suppression

Exploring the potential of CRISPR and other gene-editing technologies to target and neutralize latent viral infections like HIV and HPV.

Vaccines

mRNA Vaccine Platforms

Leveraging mRNA technology to accelerate the development of effective vaccines for herpes simplex virus (HSV) and chlamydia.

Collaboration & Partnerships

Innovation doesn't happen in a vacuum. We partner with leading research institutions, biotech startups, and global health organizations to accelerate the translation of laboratory discoveries into real-world health solutions.

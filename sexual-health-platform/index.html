Sexual Education

Comprehensive sexual education is about more than just biology. It's about rights, respect, and making informed decisions.

Consent

Consent must be voluntary, enthusiastic, specific, and can be withdrawn at any time. It's the foundation of every healthy interaction.

Healthy Relationships

Respect, communication, and trust are key components. Recognizing the signs of unhealthy dynamics is crucial for well-being.

Your Rights

Everyone has the right to sexual health services, information, and the right to live free from violence and discrimination.

STI & MST Prevention

Sexually Transmitted Infections (STIs) are common, but many are preventable and curable.

Effective Protection

Consistent and correct use of condoms is one of the most effective ways to prevent STIs, including HIV.

Regular Testing

Many STIs have no symptoms. Regular testing is the only way to know your status and protect your partners.

Curable vs. Treatable

Syphilis, gonorrhea, and chlamydia are curable with antibiotics. Others, like HIV and Herpes, are treatable with medication.

Support & Fight Against Abuse

Sexual abuse is never the victim's fault. Help and support are available.

If you are in immediate danger

Please contact your local emergency services or a trusted support organization immediately.

Visit RAINN (National Sexual Assault Hotline)

Sexual violence includes any sexual act or attempt to obtain a sexual act by violence or coercion. It is a violation of human rights.
